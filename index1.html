Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta or bacon
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Cook spaghetti. Fry pancetta with garlic. Mix eggs and cheese, then combine with pasta off heat to create creamy sauce.

Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast
  • 200ml yogurt
  • 2 tbsp tikka masala spice
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 400g crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic & ginger
  • Fresh coriander

Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices. Cook onions, garlic, & ginger, add tomatoes and simmer. Add chicken and cook through.

Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 150g butter
  • 250g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 100g flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Melt chocolate and butter. Whisk eggs and sugar, blend with chocolate mix. Fold in flour and bake until set.
